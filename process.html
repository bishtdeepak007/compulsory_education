process

AI ChatBot Messaging Platform

1

Schools securely send student information, including cell phone numbers.

2

The AllHere “EdNudge” process sends its first text introducing itself and asking students for permission to message them helpful reminders.

3

You receive a custom AllHere “Pulse” report based on how your students & families answer questions, with monthly updates throughout the summer.

4

Students & families who opt in receive a well-being survey. Based on their responses, AllHere sends “nudges” that help them stay motivated & engaged, encouraging return to school this fall.

Data Integrations

Allhere integrates with student information systems like PowerSchool to automate rostering and attendance data.

Data Security

Allhere employs industry best practices for data security and respecting policies and regulations defined in FERPA. AllHere only provides data access to authorized school or district representatives.

Service and Support
