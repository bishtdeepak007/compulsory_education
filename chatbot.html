What do you mean by Artificial Intelligence and ChatBots?

Artificial Intelligence

A flexible rational agent that can perceive its environment and takes actions that maximize its chance of success at some goal. The term “artificial intelligence” is applied when a machine mimics cognitive functions that humans associate with other human minds, such as learning and problem solving

Chatbots

A chatbot is a service, powered by rules and sometimes artificial intelligence, that you interact with via a chat (or SMS/text) interface. The service could be any number of things, ranging from functional to fun.
